About Me
Designing Is My Passion

I am Frances Bea Magdayao, the youngest, the bunso, the last puzzle piece in a family that shaped who I am. My two kuyas carved their own paths, and I watched, learning from their every move. But me? I was different. While they chased their own dreams, I found solace in creativity, in colors, in designs that spoke louder than words. They say being the youngest means being protected, but in my case, it meant finding my own way. And in that journey, I discovered that design wasn’t just an interest—it was the language my heart spoke before I even knew how to put it into words.
